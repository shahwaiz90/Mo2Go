About Us

Mo2Go Private Hire Services LTD is a small, family-run business based in the heart of Warwickshire. We pride ourselves on offering a traditional, friendly taxi service that prioritises your comfort and safety above all else.

We understand that not everyone just needs a quick lift — which is why we take extra care with elderly and less mobile passengers. From helping you to the door with your shopping, to ensuring you make it safely to hospital appointments, we look after you properly.

As locals ourselves, we know the village lanes and the quickest routes. When you book with us, you get a reliable driver who genuinely cares about making your journey pleasant and stress-free.
