# How to Set Up the WhatsApp Business Auto-Reply

Now that your website is configured to open WhatsApp with a preloaded message ("Hello Mo2Go! I would like to book a taxi."), you can set up your WhatsApp Business app to immediately send a warm greeting back to them automatically!

### Step 1: Install WhatsApp Business
If you haven't already, download the **WhatsApp Business** app from the App Store (iPhone) or Google Play Store (Android). You can convert your existing number `07365 450917` to a business account easily during setup.

### Step 2: Open Business Tools
1. Open the WhatsApp Business app.
2. Tap on **Settings** (bottom right on iPhone) or the **three-dot menu** (top right on Android).
3. Select **Business Tools**.

### Step 3: Enable the Greeting Message
1. Under the *Messaging* section, tap on **Greeting Message**.
2. Toggle the switch to turn **Send greeting message** ON.

### Step 4: Write Your Message
1. Tap on the message text box to edit it. 
2. Write a message that is helpful for someone booking a taxi. For example:
> *"Hello! 👋 Thanks for contacting Mo2Go Private Hire! How can we help you today? If you are looking to book a ride, please let us know your Pick-Up location, Destination, Date, and Time. We will get back to you momentarily to confirm!"*
3. Save the message.

### Step 5: Choose Recipients
1. Tap on **Recipients**.
2. Select **Everyone not in address book**. 
*(This ensures regular friends/family or returning saved clients don't keep getting the automated robot message, but every new customer from the website does!)*
3. Tap **Save** at the top.

That's it! Now, whenever someone clicks the floating button on your website and sends that first message, they will instantly get your automated reply asking for their journey details.
